## Summary

- Contact click analytics (already from Task 54)
- Review analytics (average, trend, response rate)
- Charts: views over time, clicks by type, rating trend
- Comparison: this week vs last week with trend indicators

- New businesses over time (line chart)
- User growth (registrations over time)
- Most active categories
- Top businesses by contact clicks
- Verification conversion rate (requested → approved)
